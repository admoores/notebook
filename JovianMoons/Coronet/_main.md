The Coronet is a massive spinning ring ship, about 6km across. Those decks facing "forward" and closer to the outer hull have a view of outer space.

The "Sun" makes a daily trip around the ring, illuminating about half of it at a time and ensuring a smooth rotation of shifts for all tasks aboard.

The 6km-radius ship makes a full revolution about every 3 minutes, maintining about 95% earth gravity.

The coronet has a version of distilled vitae running through the entire ship, as part of the main infrastructure, along with all of the networking, electricity, all that. It's meant to allow Hiram (who has been fleshcrafted into the ship.. not in-to the ship but _into_ the ship)
